Status

Description

401 Hospital Gate

Location

Athens, Greece

Status

Completed in 2021

Description

Iakovos Tsounis’ endowment allowed to the famous military hospital to renovate its entrance gate. A key consideration is the adaptation of parts –existing and new- to the whole, an appearance in unison with the rest of the hospital units and an appropriate use of ornament. All the above along with the practical needs such as the protection of the sun lead to a plain rigid form aligned to the military character of the hospital.
